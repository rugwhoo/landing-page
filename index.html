Founding Team
Superman, birth name Kal-El and adopted name Clark Kent, was a Kryptonian and the premiere superhero on Earth, often considered the first among equals of the founding members of the Justice League.
Bruce Wayne served as the CEO of Wayne Enterprises. Under the alias Batman, he secretly fought crime and injustice as a masked vigilante for many years and was a founding member of the Justice League, though he only operated as a part-timer.
Wonder Woman, known also as Diana of Themyscira, was the princess of the Amazons and one of the original seven founders of the Justice League. Wonder Woman was from Themyscira.
J'onn J'onzz was the last Martian and one of the founding members of the Justice League. J'onn can be seen as the true founder of the Justice League, having telepathically reached out to each of the founding members.
